Questions
Is the FREE trial the full version?

Yes, the 30 day free trial is the full version of Workstack.

To continue using the service after 30 days you'll be asked to sign up for a paid subscription.

 Do archived projects count to my total?

No. Only your active projects count to your overall project count.

You can archive as many projects as you like. All the project data will be stored in case you need to re-activate them.

 Is there a discount for charities or non-profits?

Yes, if you are a registered charity or a non-profit organization we offer a 25% discount to whichever plan you are on.
Do you offer annual plans?

Yes, annual plans are available and receive two months FREE. If you cancel early your unused time will not be refunded.

 What if I decide to cancel?

Your FREE trial will naturally expire and that's it, you don't need to do anything.

You only need an email address and password to get going and we only need payment details if you decide to signup for a paid plan once the trial ends.

 Where can I get answers to my questions?

If you can't find the answer to a question here then contact us, we're happy to answer any queries you might have.
